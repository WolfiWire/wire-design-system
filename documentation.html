Typography

A single family — Inter — across the product, on a compact scale tuned for dense conversation UI. Sizes are tokens (--font-size-*); weights map to --font-weight-*.

Chat bubbles — Received

Messages from others: left-aligned, --wire-bg-base-tertiary on --wire-content-base-primary, timestamp only. In channels and groups the first bubble of a stack shows the sender name and the last shows a 26px avatar; guests get a badge. One-to-one conversations drop both. Asset and width behaviour mirrors own bubbles.
